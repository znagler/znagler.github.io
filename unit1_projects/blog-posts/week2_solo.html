Site Layout

This diagram shows how I originally wanted to layout my site, but it is subject to change. My plan for all pages is to use large but not disruptive photos for backgrounds.

On the main page, I want a simple navbar across the top that links to the two blogs, the challenges, and misc. The individual blogs will have the same layout, but different backgrounds photos.

On the blogs and challenge pages, the newest post will be on the left and there will be links to old blog posts on the right. There will also be a link home on the footer inside every page
